Methods
Methods are properties containing a function definition 
        that can be performed specific to an object.
Action that can be called from an object to perform a specific task.     
    
var methods ={
  log: function(){ 
    console.log("hello I am a method");
  },
  key: function(myName){ 
    console.log("hello " + myName +" I am a method");
  },

    "apple",

    "mango"
  ],
